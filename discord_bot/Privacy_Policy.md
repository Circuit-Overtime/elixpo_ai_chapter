# Privacy Policy for Elixpo AI Chapter Discord Bot

**Effective Date:** 10.10.2024  
**Last Updated:** 31.05.2025

Thank you for using the Elixpo AI Chapter Discord Bot. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you interact with the bot on Discord.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

The bot may collect and process the following types of data:

- **Message Content:** The bot may access message content (such as prompt input, message IDs, and image attachments) to perform core features like `/edit`, `/remix`, and command referencing. Message content is only processed when required and is not stored unless temporarily needed for specific functionality (e.g. editing previously generated images).
- **Command & Usage Data:** Logs of used commands, parameters, timestamps, and errors to improve functionality and provide support.
- **Image Metadata:** Basic metadata about images submitted or generated (e.g., aspect ratio, prompt, seed, or model selected) to enable accurate image processing.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

Collected data is used to:

- Provide, maintain, and improve bot functionality.
- Allow advanced features like editing or remixing based on previously generated images.
- Support troubleshooting, performance monitoring, and usage analytics.
- Ensure compliance with Discord's Terms of Service and community guidelines.

---

## 3. Data Storage & Security

- We do **not** sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties.
- Temporary data (such as image generation context) is stored securely and only accessed by authorized maintainers.
- Message content is **not** stored long-term unless required by a bot feature and will be regularly purged or deleted upon user request.
- All reasonable measures are taken to prevent unauthorized access to stored data.

---

## 4. Third-Party Services

The bot may use external APIs or services (e.g., for AI image generation and remixing). Only the minimum necessary data is shared with these services, and it is subject to their own privacy policies. Examples include AI models like `flux`, `turbo`, or `gptimage`.

---

## 5. Use of Message Intents

To support features like `/edit` and `/remix`, the bot requires access to message content and message references. This access is strictly used to locate prior images or messages the user interacted with and to fulfill the intended command. We do **not** read or analyze unrelated messages and never use this access for advertising, tracking, or profiling users.

---

## 6. Children’s Privacy

This bot is not intended for children under the age of 10. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under this age.

---

## 7. Your Rights

- You may request deletion of your command or usage data at any time. Contact the maintainers via GitHub or Discord.
- You may remove the bot from your server at any time, which stops all data processing and interaction.
- You may request a summary of your stored data if applicable.

---

## 8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be published in the bot's GitHub repository and/or announced via Discord.

---
